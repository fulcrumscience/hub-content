# Biology Datasets

Benchmark datasets for drug discovery, protein science, and computational biology.

## Drug Discovery

| Dataset | Task | Size | Link |
|---------|------|------|------|
| **TDC** | Drug discovery benchmarks | Various | [tdcommons.ai](https://tdcommons.ai/) |
| **MoleculeNet** | ADMET, toxicity | 700K+ molecules | [Paper](https://pubs.rsc.org/en/content/articlelanding/2018/sc/c7sc02664a) |
| **ChEMBL** | Bioactivity data | 2M+ compounds | [ebi.ac.uk/chembl](https://www.ebi.ac.uk/chembl/) |
