# Biology Datasets

Benchmark datasets for drug discovery, protein science, and computational biology.

## Drug Discovery

| Dataset | Task | Size | Link |
|---------|------|------|------|
| **TDC** | Drug discovery benchmarks | Various | [tdcommons.ai](https://tdcommons.ai/) |
| **MoleculeNet** | ADMET, toxicity | 700K+ molecules | [moleculenet.ai](http://moleculenet.ai/) |
| **ChEMBL** | Bioactivity data | 2M+ compounds | [ebi.ac.uk/chembl](https://www.ebi.ac.uk/chembl/) |
| **BindingDB** | Drug-target binding data | 2.6M+ entries | [bindingdb.org](https://www.bindingdb.org/) |
| **PDBbind** | Protein-ligand affinity | 23K+ complexes | [pdbbind.org.cn](http://www.pdbbind.org.cn/) |
| **DrugBank** | Approved and investigational drugs | 15K+ drugs | [drugbank.com](https://www.drugbank.com/) |
| **ZINC** | Virtual screening compounds | 250M+ | [zinc.docking.org](https://zinc.docking.org/) |
