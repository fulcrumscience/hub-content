# Materials Resources

Courses, teaching materials, and learning resources for materials informatics.

## Courses

### Machine Learning for Materials
**[aronwalsh.github.io/MLforMaterials](https://aronwalsh.github.io/MLforMaterials/Overview.html)**

**Institution:** Imperial College London | **Level:** Graduate

Introduction to statistical research tools for materials theory and simulation.

**Topics covered:**
- Regression and classification
- Feature engineering for materials
- Neural network potentials
- Uncertainty quantification
