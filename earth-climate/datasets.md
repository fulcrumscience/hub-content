# Earth & Climate Datasets

Datasets for climate modeling, carbon flux monitoring, and environmental research.

## Carbon Flux & Ecosystem Data

| Dataset | Description | Link |
|---------|-------------|------|
| **Ameriflux** | Carbon, water, and energy flux data across the Americas | [ml4sci](https://ml4sci.lbl.gov/ameriflux) |
| **FLUXNET2015** | Global network of carbon flux measurements | [ml4sci](https://ml4sci.lbl.gov/fluxnet2015) |
| **FLUXNET LaThuile** | Earlier carbon flux synthesis dataset | [ml4sci](https://ml4sci.lbl.gov/fluxnet_lathuile) |
| **ESS-Dive** | Environmental Systems Science Data Infrastructure | [ml4sci](https://ml4sci.lbl.gov/essdive) |
